Live Webinar: Learn to "Speak Predix" in 60 minutes

Wednesday, April 9
Two session times offered: 8:00 AM EDT & 8:00 PM EDT

Predix is GE’s software platform for the Industrial Internet. Predix enables asset and operations optimization by providing a standard way to run industrial-scale analytics and connect machines, data, and people. Deployed on machines, on-premise, or in the cloud, Predix combines an industry-leading stack of technologies for distributed computing and big data analytics, asset management, machine-to-machine communication.

Attend this 60-minute webinar to:

  • Learn what Predix is and how it can help you
  • Understand product status, direction and positioning
  • Hear read-life case studies of "Predix at work"
This internal-only webinar is an introduction overview for GE business and technical stakeholders.
